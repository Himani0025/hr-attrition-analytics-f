## HR Employee Attrition & Performance Analytics

**Tools:** Excel | SQL (Window Functions, CTEs) | Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Scipy) | Tableau Public
**Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics — 1,470 employees, 35 variables (Kaggle)

## Business Problem
Identify which factors statistically drive employee attrition
and provide HR with actionable retention recommendations.

## Key Findings
- Overtime workers leave at 30.5% vs 10.4% — nearly 3× higher (p < 0.0001)
- Employees who left earned $1,300/month less than those who stayed
- Job satisfaction score 1 has 22.8% attrition vs 11.3% at score 4
- Department alone is NOT statistically significant (p = 0.40)
